### PDPO Compliance Framework Summary

**6 Data Protection Principles (DPPs):**
1. **DPP1:** Lawful and fair collection with purpose notification
2. **DPP2:** Accuracy and limited retention (Section 26: erase when no longer needed)
3. **DPP3:** Use only for original purpose (Part 6A: informed consent for direct marketing)
4. **DPP4:** Data security - protect against unauthorized access
5. **DPP5:** Openness and transparency of policies
6. **DPP6:** Access and correction rights

**Key Penalties:**
- DPP violations ‚Üí Enforcement notice (non-compliance: HK$50,000 + 2 years imprisonment)
- Section 26 (failure to erase): HK$10,000 fine
- Direct marketing violations: HK$500,000 - HK$1,000,000 + 3-5 years imprisonment
- Doxxing offences: Criminal prosecution

**Enforcement:**
- Privacy Commissioner can investigate, issue enforcement notices, prosecute
- Data subjects can seek civil compensation
- Commissioner may provide legal assistance
