### Key Findings from AI Overview

**Six Data Protection Principles (DPPs):**
1. **DPP 1: Lawful and fair collection** - Collect through lawful/fair means, inform data subjects of purpose
2. **DPP 2: Accuracy and retention** - Ensure accuracy, up-to-date, retain only as necessary
3. **DPP 3: Use and purpose** - Use only for original purpose or with prescribed consent; "Use" includes disclosure and transfer
4. **DPP 4: Security** - Protect from unauthorized/accidental access, processing, erasure, loss, or use
5. **DPP 5: Openness** - Be open about data held and main purposes
6. **DPP 6: Access and correction** - Give data subjects access and allow correction of inaccuracies

**Additional Compliance Requirements:**
- **Direct marketing:** Obtain informed and explicit consent before using/transferring data
- **Data access requests:** Keep logbook of all refusals with reasons
- **Internal policies:** Establish and periodically review policies for data governance and security
- **Data breach notification:** Recommended (not always mandatory) to notify Privacy Commissioner
- **Doxxing:** Criminal offense; Privacy Commissioner has powers to investigate and prosecute
