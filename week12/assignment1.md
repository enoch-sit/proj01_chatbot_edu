# INT6137 Individual Assignment

## Overview

**Individual assignment (50% out of the final grade of the course):**  
A critical analysis of the chatbot's performance, with suggestions for improvement, should be written in 1500 words.

---

## Course Information

### Assignment Description

Students are required to submit a **1500-word critical analysis report** evaluating the performance of their group-developed chatbot in a specific organizational context (e.g., healthcare, law, education, finance, domestic, or customer service).

The report must include:

- Evidence-based analysis of strengths and weaknesses, supported by data
- Actionable suggestions for improvement
- Analysis drawing on course concepts such as:
  - Agentic AI
  - Agile/Scrum methodologies
  - Legal aspects/liabilities

### Submission Requirements

- **Word count:** 1500 words (excluding references and appendices)
- **Structure:**
  - Title page
  - Introduction
  - Critical analysis
  - Suggestions for improvement
  - Conclusion
  - References (APA 7)
  - Optional appendices
- **Due Date:** 17 December 2025
- **Weighting:** 50% of the final grade
